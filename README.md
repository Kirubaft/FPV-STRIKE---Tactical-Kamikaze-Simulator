🚁 FPV STRIKE - Tactical Kamikaze Simulator
FPV STRIKE is a hardcore kamikaze FPV drone simulator with realistic flight physics, total object destruction, and an economy system. Built on web technologies (Electron + Three.js).
🔥 Main Features
Realistic flight physics: Angle mode simulation taking into account gravity, inertia, air resistance, and tilt angle.
Total destruction (Debris Engine): Custom debris physics engine. Tanks are torn apart into turrets and tracks, houses shatter into bricks, and trees — into logs.
Artificial Intelligence (AI): Infantry reacts to the approaching drone, looks at the sky, panics, and runs to hide under trees.
Recording system (DVR Replays): The game automatically records videos of your flights. Watch your best hits right in the main menu!
Economy and Garage: Complete missions, earn money, and buy new, more powerful drones (from the light Scout to the heavy bomber BABA YAGA).
Dynamic sound: Engine sound is procedurally generated via the Web Audio API and depends on speed and thrust.
Multilingual: Support for over 80 languages.
🎮 Controls
The controls are optimized for the keyboard to provide maximum control:
W / S — Tilt forward / backward (Pitch)
A / D — Bank left / right (Roll)
Q / E — Turn camera (Yaw)
SHIFT — Throttle / Gain altitude (Throttle Up)
SPACE — Cut throttle / Descend (Throttle Down)
Tip: To fly forward without losing altitude, hold down W and SHIFT simultaneously. To destroy a target — just fly into it at speed!
